Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Fintro is operated by Louis Fritz, Schwyz, Switzerland

🔒

Short version: Fintro collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no tracking, no cookies, and no data is ever sent to a server. Everything stays on your device.

📋 What data we collect

Fintro collects no personal data whatsoever. We don't ask for your name, email address, age, or any other identifying information.

The only data stored is your learning progress (XP, completed chapters, language preference), saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

Fintro uses no cookies and no tracking technologies of any kind. There is no analytics software, no advertising pixels, no fingerprinting, and no third-party tracking scripts.

The only external service loaded is Google Fonts (for typography). Google may log this request as part of their standard CDN operation — we have no control over this, but no personal data from your usage of Fintro is involved.

🧒 Children & Minors

Fintro is specifically designed for teenagers. Because we collect no personal data and require no account creation, the platform is safe for users of all ages, including minors under 13 (COPPA) and under 16 (GDPR).

Parents and guardians can verify this policy at any time. No consent mechanism is required because no data is collected.

📤 Data Sharing

We share no data with any third parties — because we collect none. There are no advertising partners, data brokers, or analytics companies involved with Fintro.

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

Your progress is stored only in your browser's localStorage. To delete it completely:

  • Open your browser settings
  • Go to "Clear browsing data" or "Storage"
  • Clear site data for fintrooo.netlify.app (or your current Fintro URL)

Alternatively, clearing all localStorage data in your browser will remove Fintro's data along with any other sites.

📬 Contact

Fintro is a personal project built and operated by Louis Fritz, age 14, based in Schwyz, Switzerland. For any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the contact information on the About page.

This website is non-commercial and free. It is not operated as a business.
